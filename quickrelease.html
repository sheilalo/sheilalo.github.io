Formula One Quick Release Mechanism September 2015 - November 2015

Overview

As part of MECE09029: Computer Aided Engineering 3, I reverse engineered and modeled a Formula One quick release mechanism and steering wheel.

Exploded view of quick release mechanism

Purpose

The course served to familiarize students with different tools in Solid Edge including: parametric modeling, assemblies, finite elements, optimization, curves and surfaces, direct/synchronous modeling, standard parts, rendering and animation, and drawings. This class culminated in a final report evaluating Solid Edge CAD tools and showcasing our quick release mechanisms.

Final Design
