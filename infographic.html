Youth Vote Infographic October 2016 - November 2016

Overview

As part of DSGN 420: Design Communication and Methods, students created infographics and video animations to encourage young adults to vote.

Final Design

My goal was to dismiss the myth that the youth vote “doesn’t matter” and show that we can greatly impact our government by voting. I used a color scheme that played off of patriotic colors, while using arrows to maintain a steady pace in the presentation of information.
