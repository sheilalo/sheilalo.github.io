Floodlight MS

Background

The Floodlight MS smartphone app is an EU MDR-certified medical device that collects objective data on the function of patients with multiple sclerosis between clinic visits with their healthcare provider. While at Roche, I collaborated with the human factors and design teams to conduct summative and clinical validation studies for the Floodlight program. I also supported the EU MDR gap assessment and audit as part of the Regulatory team.

Validation Studies

Because of COVID-19, I remotely supported our on-site moderator in conducting 1:1 simulated use testing with a set of representative participants. Then, I transcribed data to create a heat map of the users' performance on tasks to conduct a root cause analysis. In addition, I contributed to authoring the summative study reports and usability engineering files.
