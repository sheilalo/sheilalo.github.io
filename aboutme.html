Hi there! I’m Sheila, a first year graduate student at Northwestern University pursuing an MS in Engineering Design Innovation. I graduated from the California Institute of Technology in June 2016 with a BS in Mechanical Engineering.

My experiences in engineering design and emergency medicine have inspired me to pursue human-centered design. Ergonomics are important. I want to find elegant solutions to hard problems in order to make user experience more intuitive and accessible. My goal as a human-centered design engineer is to be the user’s advocate, to ultimately create products that people both can and want to use.
